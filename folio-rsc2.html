PORTAL REDESIGN

RS Components

ROLE: Lead UX Designer
DATE: Sep 2010 - Mar 2012
CLIENT: RS Components
TOOLS: Axure, AI, PS, FW
Challenge

RS Components (Radio Shack in US) is one of the largest distributers of electronic components globally. When I met them they had a problem with shopping cart abandonment. Nearly 50% of all items placed in the basket just sat there. No one knew exactly why the conversion rate was so low. But it was easy to see that their site was dated.

What I Did

I led the user-centered design process - beginning with analysing research studies and ending with functional prototypes. Together with the lead designer inside the company we re-imagined signature journeys during regular brain-storming sessions. Through iterative rounds of wire frames, visual mockups, and HTML prototypes we solved many interesting problems.

Impact

After 18 months of hard work, we had redesigned the entire portal. RS reported that shopping cart abandonment had been cut in half. Also, customers reported significantly great satisfaction with the experience because they had new ways to discover, compare, verify, and manage component parts they needed.
