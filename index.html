About Liz

I'm a UX Designer. My career has spanned two decades and followed the evolution of technology. I've delivered large-scale web and mobile products for big brands such as Amazon, Microsoft, Vodafone, and RS Components.

Most recently, I served as an Alexa Evangelist and was the first person to build skills with developers in UK and Germany. I excelled in this role because it required my best skills: ideation, prototyping, coaching, and language (I speak German and Spanish fluently.)

Ultimately, voice UI has led to designing for IoT, smart home, and augmented reality too. Currently, I'm learning python and processing (so that I can visualize data in my lantern project). In my next role, I want to join a team designing for voice, smart home, and/or mixed reality.
